ABOUT

I’m passionate about technology and curious about how software shapes everyday experiences. I enjoy playing video games, which sparked my interest in how applications and systems are built behind the scenes. Exploring code, experimenting with designs, and learning new tools have become hobbies that continuously push me to improve my skills. I like solving problems and finding creative ways to turn ideas into working solutions. Every project I work on is an opportunity to grow and better understand the world of development.

Professionally, I aspire to become a software developer and eventually a software engineer who builds reliable, efficient, and user-friendly applications. I hope to work on projects that challenge me while allowing me to contribute meaningful solutions. My goal is to keep learning, improving, and creating software that makes a positive impact.
